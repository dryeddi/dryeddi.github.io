Dr. Yeddi Park

Psychotherapist



About

I have been in clinical practice for over fifteen years. I believe a key to good therapeutic outcome starts with trust and connection. I use a client-centered approach and my clinical orientation combines psychodynamic, cognitive, and relational approaches. I received my Ph.D. from New York University.


I am a practitioner and scholar in the field of mental health, and have published numerous manuscripts in peer-reviewed academic journals. My practice and research interests include depression, immigrant families, integenerational issues, and minority psychology with a particular focus on East Asian American experiences. I am bilingual in English and Korean.
